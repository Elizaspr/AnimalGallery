New Zealand Rabbit

Caring for a New Zealand Rabbit:

SPACE: As a fairly larger breed, they need more space then others. So expect to build a custom enclosure!

Health Issues: Like all rabbits they are prone to a few health issues like Obesity, Dental Issues, and Sore Hocks.

Food: Rabbits love to eat and need to forage constantly. They need Hay, Leafy Green Veggies, and Pellets that are given daily
